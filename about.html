About Winsome Model Schools

Founded in 1995, Winsome Model Schools has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in Ifako, Lagos, Nigeria. Registered with WAEC and NECO, we are committed to preparing students for global success through innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art facilities, and a nurturing environment that fosters both academic and personal growth.

Our journey began with a vision to redefine education in Nigeria, focusing on holistic development, ethical values, and academic rigor. Our History

1995 - Founding Year

Winsome Model Schools was established with 50 students and a small team of dedicated educators.

2000 - First WAEC Success

Our first set of students achieved a 95% pass rate in WAEC, marking the beginning of our academic excellence.

2005 - Campus Expansion

We expanded our campus to include modern science labs, a library, and a sports complex.

2010 - National Recognition

Winsome Model Schools received the National Education Excellence Award for outstanding performance.

2015 - 20th Anniversary

Celebrated 20 years of educational excellence with a grand alumni reunion and community outreach programs.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Introduced e-learning platforms and smart classrooms to enhance the learning experience during the pandemic.

2025 - 30th Anniversary

Marking 30 years of shaping futures with new initiatives, including a scholarship program for underprivileged students.
