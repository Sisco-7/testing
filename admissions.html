Admissions

Join Winsome Model Schools and embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and supportive, ensuring every applicant has the best chance to succeed.

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds, offering scholarships and financial aid to those in need. Follow the steps below to begin your application today.

Admission Process

Step 1: Application Form

Purchase and complete the application form online or at our admissions office.

Cost: ₦5,000

Download Form

Step 2: Submit Documents

Submit the required documents, including birth certificate, school records, and passport photographs.

Deadline: Rolling admissions

Upload Documents

Step 3: Entrance Exam

Attend the entrance exam, which tests core subjects like Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge.

Exam Date: Monthly

Schedule Exam

Step 4: Interview

Participate in an interview with our admissions panel to assess fit and potential.

Duration: 30 minutes

Book Interview

Step 5: Registration

Complete registration by paying the acceptance fee and enrolling in classes.

Acceptance Fee: ₦50,000

Pay Fee

Admission Requirements

Junior Secondary (JSS1-JSS3)

  • Birth Certificate
  • Primary School Records
  • Passport Photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Report
  • Application Fee Receipt

Senior Secondary (SS1-SS3)

  • Birth Certificate
  • JSS Certificate or Transfer Certificate
  • Passport Photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Report
  • Application Fee Receipt

Transfer Students

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Records
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Passport Photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Report
  • Application Fee Receipt

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We offer various scholarships and financial aid options to support deserving students.

  • Merit Scholarship: For students with outstanding academic performance (up to 50% fee waiver).
  • Need-Based Aid: For families with demonstrated financial need (up to 30% fee waiver).
  • Sports Scholarship: For exceptional athletes (up to 20% fee waiver).
  • Community Service Award: For students with significant community involvement (up to 15% fee waiver).
